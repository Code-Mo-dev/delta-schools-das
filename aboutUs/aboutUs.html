Our Founders

Chairman, Board of Trustees

Dr. Mohamed Rabie Nasser

Dr. Mohamed Rabie Nasser is a pioneer of private education in Egypt. He serves as Chairman of the Board of Trustees at Delta University for Science and Technology and is the founder of Delta Academy and Sphinx and Nile Valley Universities. He is known for his initiatives to support these efforts, most notably the Mohamed Rabie Award for Scientific Research, which recognizes outstanding researchers in the fields of medicine, technology, and artificial intelligence.

Dean, Delta Higher Institute

Dr. Ahmed Abu El-Fotouh

Dr. Ahmed Abu El-Fotouh is an academic professor and Dean of the Delta Higher Institute for Computer Science and Management Information Systems in Mansoura. He is a key educational leader in the "Delta" group and is responsible for the technical and administrative supervision of the institute's engineering departments and the development of the scientific research system.

Security Expert & Senior Academic

Maj. Gen. Dr. Abdul Wahab Al-Ra'i

Major General Dr. Abdul Wahab Al-Ra'i is a distinguished security expert and academic with a 30-year career in the Ministry of Interior, specializing in organized crime with a 99% conviction rate. A recipient of the Presidential Medal of Excellence, he now serves as a senior academic and vice-dean, and is a recognized authority on intellectual security and legal legislation.
