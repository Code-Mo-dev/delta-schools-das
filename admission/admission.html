Admission Requirements for Delta American School (2026)

Delta American School follows standard procedures for international schools in Egypt, with specific requirements tailored to each academic stage. Below is a list of the essential documents and procedures required for enrollment.

Basic Documents Required for all stages

  • Birth Certificate: Original "Computerized" birth certificate (recent).
  • Personal Photos: 8 recent passport-sized photos of the student (white background).
  • ID Copies: Valid National ID copies of both parents (Father & Mother).
  • Educational Qualifications: Copies of university degrees for both parents.
  • Passport (For Non-Egyptians): Copies of the student's and parents' passports with a valid residency permit.

Transfer Students

  • Success Certificate: An official transcript of the last academic year, stamped by the Educational Directorate.
  • Student File: Withdrawal of the student's file from the previous school (after receiving the acceptance letter).
  • Certificate of Good Conduct: Sometimes required for Middle and High School students.

Admission Procedures

  • Application Form: Fill out the form via the school's website or at the school premises.
  • Assessment: Schedule an entrance exam for the student in English, Mathematics, and Arabic.
  • Parent Interview: A personal interview for the parents with the school administration.

It is always recommended to contact the school's Admissions Department directly to confirm deadlines, as the school may require specific updates or additional paperwork based on new Ministry of Education regulations.
