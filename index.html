Delta American Schools – Mansoura

Delta American Schools in Mansoura is a leading educational institution dedicated to providing high-quality American-based education. With over 25 years of experience, the school combines global teaching standards with a supportive and inclusive learning environment.

Vision

To be recognized as a center of educational excellence that inspires lifelong learning, critical thinking, and global citizenship.

Mission

Our mission is to deliver an outstanding American curriculum that nurtures creativity, confidence, and character in every student.
